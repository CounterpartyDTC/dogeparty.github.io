Why Dogeparty?

Make a smart comment over on reddit.com/r/dogecoin to get tips from users, try an exchange like Cryptsy, or check out Dogecoin Faucets.

What Can I Do with Dogeparty?

A lot, actually. Making your own token on Dogeparty means you can have your own asset to distribute, while still supporting the Dogecoin network and community. Raise funds in DOGE, and reward your supporters with a custom coin that can be tailored to your needs, without needing your own blockchain!

  • Create

    Want to publish a book or album without a publisher or have an amazing product ? Create your own token, put up a page explaining your goals and collect DOGE donations from your supporters! You get the funds, your backers get a token that can give them access or a share of the project. When you're ready, reward your backers by trading your work for the coin you gave out.

  • Plan events

    Planning an actual Doge Party, or even just a normal human event? Raise funds for the work and costs by selling an EVENT coin that can then be used at the event to pay for entry, drinks, and shibe snacks. You get the funds up front, and your guests get something tangible (and fungible) in return. This allows you to cover costs, and also give your backers a guaranteed unit of value beyond a name on the guestlist. In fact, the coin IS the guestlist!

  • Give back

    Raising funds for doing good? Create a CharityCoin to give back to donors who support your cause. You can then make the token valuable by having businesses and supporters donate and services which are auctioned for the CharityCoin at one-off or periodic events where only the CharityCoin is accepted.

What is XDP?

Dogeparty uses XDP, a coin that can be controlled by the Dogeparty protocol. This means that XDP can do some things that are impossible for a standard Dogecoin. Most importantly the Dogeparty protocol itself can be the trusted third party in a variety of ways.

For example, when someone places an order to sell XDP, or places a bet, or buys a Dogeparty-created asset using XDP, the funds for their order are held by the Dogecoin blockchain, enabling easy, fast, and trustless interactions. So, while Dogeparty runs on the power of DOGE, you'll need XDP to do awesome things on Dogeparty.
